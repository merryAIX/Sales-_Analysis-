# Sales_Analysis 

## Project Overview
This interactive Power BI dashboard provides a deep-dive analysis into the sales and financial health of an e-commerce enterprise from 2014 to 2017. The project transforms over 9,900 transactional records into an executive-level visual tool designed to optimize net profit margins, monitor regional sales distribution, and audit order-level efficiency.

## Key Financial KPIs & Metrics
* **Total Revenue Generated:** $2,297,201
* **Total Net Profit:** $286,397
* **Net Profit Margin:** 12%
* **Total Volume:** 6,144 Orders across 9,994 Transactions.
* **Total Strategic Discounts Applied:** $1,561

---

## Dashboard Architecture & Features

### 1. Executive Summary (Page 1)
Provides a high-level overview of enterprise health. The page utilizes a custom, compact canvas layout to maximize visual density and maintain stakeholder focus on primary financial indicators.
* **Key Visuals:** Top-tier KPI Cards, Volume by Category (Vertical Bar Chart), Sales Distribution by Region, and Profit Contribution by Customer Segment.

## Actionable Business Insights Delivered
* **Geographical Dominance:** California and Texas stand out as the primary revenue drivers, indicating key geographic markets for targeted growth and inventory allocation.
* **Operational Audit Readiness:** Replaced raw, unmanageable transaction logs with a clean, sortable Order ID & Profit Table, enabling management to instantly audit and track specific high-value, high-profit transactions.
* **Product Category Focus:** "Office Supplies" consistently leads the order volume, highlighting a stable baseline demand that can be leveraged for strategic cross-selling.


## Technical Competencies Demonstrated
* **Data Transformation:** Utilized Power Query to clean, shape, and structure the underlying e-commerce datasets.
* **Advanced Analytics:** Implemented explicit DAX measures for profit margins, transaction counts, and custom business metrics.
* **UI/UX Design Best Practices:** Applied rigorous professional design standards, including consistent corporate color palettes, explicit currency formatting, proper data-to-ink ratio, and precise canvas sizing for production environments.
